Our Profile

Established in 2014, Naachimar Exports is a reckoned business entity making available a wide range of products in the global markets. Situated in Karur, Tamil Nadu, the company has spread its arms in almost all parts of the globe. Under the proficient headship of the honourable proprietor, N Gowtham, the company has been scaling new heights of success in all its business endeavours. Owing to his vast experience, we are capable of catering to the exact expectations of the clients

Strengths

We have advanced facilities for storage, processing and packaging of the assorted products that we deal in. Besides the facilities our team is the key strength. We are assisted by a team of professionals who are well versed with the demands of the clients and procure the best quality from the most dependable vendors. Further our team comprises professionals from various domains including marketing, quality assurance etc. Hence we ensure delivery of only flawless products.

Quality Assurance

Quality is the maker concern of our company. Hence to ensure the best quality, we conduct stringent tests and checks on all the products following strict quality regulations. All the checks are conducted under the supervision of our quality control executives. This enables us to deliver premium products to the clients, meeting their diverse requirements.

Network

We have expanded our base in different parts of the world. Our network base is largely based out of Sharjah, Singapore and U.K. We aspire to satisfy clients to the fullest. Thus, we make efficient and time bound delivery with the support of our well connected network.
